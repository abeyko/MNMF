Isolinear Chips

2016.08.06

Programmed the following game in Arduino. Wired up LEDs to reed switches, that light up when a magnetic chip is inserted in the correct location on the board. When the magnet is brought into close contact with the reed switch, the reeds pull together to complete the circuit and the LED lights up.
